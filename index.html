About Me

Hi there! My name is Keven Benitez and I am a student currently enrolled in the UCLA Coding Bootcamp program, where I am learning the ins and outs of full stack web development. I have always been fascinated by the world of technology and the way it has transformed the way we live and work. I decided to pursue a career in web development because I believe it offers endless opportunities to create innovative solutions that can help people in their everyday lives. In the UCLA Coding Bootcamp, I am gaining hands-on experience with all aspects of web development, from front-end design using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, to back-end development with Node.js, Express, and MongoDB. I am also learning how to use popular web development frameworks like React and Redux. The program is challenging, but incredibly rewarding. I am constantly pushing myself to learn new skills and tackle complex problems, and I am excited to see how far I can go.
